Abstract
 The site discusses about how virtual reality (VR) games have evolved over the years. It started with some attempts in the 1980s, faced challenges, but gained more interest in the 1990s. The real breakthrough came in the 2010s with the release of consumer-ready VR hardware, like the Oculus Rift in 2016. This marked a significant moment for VR games. Major games, such as "Half-Life: Alyx," further boosted the popularity of VR gaming. The article shows the journey from early struggles to recent advancements that shaped the history of VR games.
