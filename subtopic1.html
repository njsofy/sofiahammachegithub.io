Abstract
 Site contains a basic timeline that traces the evolution of the video game industry from its early stages in the 1950s to the golden age of arcade games in the late 1970s and early 1980s. It discusses key developments such as the creation of early computer games like Spacewar! and the emergence of arcade games like Pong.

 The History of Pong
Site Author
 Pong Contributors
Abstract
 This site provides a historical overview of the early days of video games, outlining significant moments in the late 1970s and early 1980s, including the Space Invaders arcade game, the founding of Activision, the introduction of Pac-Man, and the impact of Nintendo with the NES in 1985. The discussion extends to the video game crash of 1983 and Nintendo's resurgence with the Game Boy in 1989, marking the beginning of handheld gaming.
